IM is a community of makers, artists, designers, developers, coders, hackers, producers, educators, and creative technologists. IM is about creating experiences for people that are delightful, insightful, magical, critical, unsettling, encouraging, and thought provoking. IM explores the ways we connect, participate, collaborate, and communicate with one another. IM involves making, breaking, sharing, playing, presenting, reflecting, repeating, rewinding and revising. IM is art, design, communications, engineering, computer science, and more.
